Touch a landmark, come on, I know you want to…

Myself and designer Karen Nguyen decided to collaborate on an interactive 3D printed model of Manhattan.
Equipped with capacitive touch sensors in front of each printed structure the user can take a tour of
Manhattan and see some of the most iconic landmarks as well as our favorite places to go. Each time someone
touches a sensor they can read a quote that you would “only hear in New York” to complete this unique way to see the city.
